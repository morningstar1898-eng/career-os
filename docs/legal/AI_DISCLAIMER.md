# AI Disclaimer

- Job fit scores are **guidance based on keyword and profile comparison**, not
  guarantees of fit, interview, or offer.
- Skill-gap findings use hedged language ("possible gap", "likely improvement
  area") because they are inferred from job descriptions vs. your profile —
  they can be wrong.
- Drafted resumes, cover letters, and replies must be **reviewed by you**
  before use. The product is designed never to invent experience; you are
  responsible for final accuracy.
- Lessons and recommended resources may become outdated; prefer official
  documentation where linked.
- Email classification is probabilistic. Confidence scores are shown, low
  confidence events do not change your pipeline, and every automatic change is
  recorded in your application history where you can correct it.
- The product does not provide legal, financial, or immigration advice.
