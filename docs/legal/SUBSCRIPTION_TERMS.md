# Subscription Terms (draft — review with counsel before public launch)

- **Trial**: 3 days, full trial-tier features, **no credit card required**.
  When the trial ends you keep your account, your data, export, and deletion —
  paid features pause until you subscribe.
- **Plans**: Starter, Pro, Premium (see the in-app plan page for the current
  feature and limit matrix). Limits are per-day and enforced server-side.
- **Billing**: processed by Stripe. We store your Stripe customer and
  subscription IDs, never your card number. Subscription state comes only from
  verified Stripe webhooks.
- **Cancellation**: cancel any time via the billing portal; access continues
  through the paid period. A failed payment triggers a grace period
  (past_due) before access downgrades.
- **Refunds**: [set your policy before launch].
- **Changes**: plan features and prices may change with notice; changes never
  apply retroactively to an already-paid period.
