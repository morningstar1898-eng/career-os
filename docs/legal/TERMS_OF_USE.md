# Terms of Use (draft — review with counsel before public launch)

1. **What this product is.** A human-in-the-loop AI career assistant: it finds
   job opportunities, scores fit, drafts materials, tracks your pipeline, and
   recommends skills to build. **It does not guarantee employment, interviews,
   or any outcome.**

2. **You apply, not us.** The product does not submit applications without
   your explicit, per-application approval. Statuses like "Applied" reflect
   actions you confirmed or events detected from your connected email.

3. **AI content may be wrong.** Fit scores, skill-gap analyses, lessons, and
   drafted materials are guidance generated by software and may be inaccurate
   or outdated. Review everything before relying on it.

4. **Truthful materials are your responsibility.** The product is designed to
   never invent experience, but you are responsible for the accuracy of every
   application you submit. Do not use the product to misrepresent yourself.

5. **Acceptable use.** No mass-applying, no circumventing job-board terms or
   technical protections, no submitting others' data, no attempting to access
   other users' data.

6. **Subscriptions.** The free trial lasts the advertised period with no card
   required. Paid plans renew until cancelled; entitlements are enforced
   server-side. See docs/legal/SUBSCRIPTION_TERMS.md.

7. **Termination.** Accounts violating these terms may be suspended. You can
   delete your account and data at any time.
